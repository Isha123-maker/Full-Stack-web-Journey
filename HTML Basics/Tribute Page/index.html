Why Women in Tech Matter

Women have made major contributions to the tech world—often without getting the spotlight they deserve. From early pioneers like Ada Lovelace to today’s AI leaders, they’ve helped shape our digital future. Their ideas, innovations, and leadership bring diversity, creativity, and new problem-solving perspectives to the world of technology.

List of Notable Women in Tech

  • Ada Lovelace– First computer programmer ever
  • Grace Hopper – Invented the first compiler; queen of debugging
  • Radia Perlman – Invented the protocol that made the internet work
  • Fei-Fei Li– Leader in AI and computer vision
  • Reshma Saujani – Founded Girls Who Code
  • Kimberly Bryant – Empowering Black girls in tech with Black Girls Code
